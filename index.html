How it works
1. The original video audio is decoded via Web Audio API
2. A metronome click track is synthesized in PCM
3. Both tracks are mixed at your chosen levels → written as WAV
4. FFmpeg.wasm muxes: original video frames (bit-perfect copy) + new audio → MP4

Note: FFmpeg.wasm (~30 MB) loads on first use. Video quality is lossless — only audio is re-encoded to AAC.
